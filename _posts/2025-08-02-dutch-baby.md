# Batter

Add to a blender:

* 1 cup all-purpose **flour**
* 1 cup whole or 2% **milk**
* 4 large **eggs**
* 4 tbsp granulated **sugar**
* 1 tsp kosher **salt**
* 2 tsp **vanilla extract**

Blend for **10 seconds**.

Scrape sides and blend again. The batter should be loose and liquidy -- ready to pour.

Rest in blender for 20 min. While you wait, preheat the oven and pan.


# Cook

Remove any racks above the middle oven rack and put a 9 to 10-inch cast iron skillet on the middle rack.

Heat the oven to 425Â°F.

Add to the pan and melt:

* 4 tbsp unsalted **butter**

Pour in the batter, tilt the pan to coat the bottom evenly, and put in the oven. Bake for **15-20 minutes**.

Serve with:

* Powdered sugar, maple syrup, and jam, for serving
